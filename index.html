Introduction





PIA_5002 is a virtual airline in airlines manager. PIA_5002 represents Pakistan International Airlines, the national flag carrier of Islamic Republic of Pakistan. PIA_5002 was created in airlines manager in the mid of 2019. Currently, PIA_5002 is a member of Atlas Alliance. PIA_5002 have account in tycoon mode in airlines manager. PIA_5002 main hubs are ISB, LYP, AYT and MED. PIA_5002 secondary hubs are JFK, HGK,LHR, DXB, FRA, BKK and KUL.
